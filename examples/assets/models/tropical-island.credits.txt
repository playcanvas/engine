Tropical Island
===============

Title:   Tropical Island
Author:  Elin (https://sketchfab.com/ElinHohler)

Modifications (made for use in the PlayCanvas examples):
- Removed the skybox mesh and the bone-animated water plane mesh (including its
  armature, skin, animation, material and textures) - the PlayCanvas Water script
  renders the water instead.
- Removed the KHR_materials_unlit flag from all materials, so the island is lit
  dynamically by the scene lighting.
- Removed the baked shadows from the beach texture.
- Extended the beach mesh with a generated skirt - its open boundary extruded outwards,
  sloping down into the distance, so the terrain continues under water.
- Resized all textures to half their original resolution and converted them to WebP.
- Applied Draco mesh compression.
